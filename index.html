Agenda

Research

My research is organized around reliable embodied systems: how robots perceive, reason, adapt, and remain safe when operating in precise physical environments.

01

Scientific Robotics Safety

Building high-fidelity chemical-lab benchmarks, automated expert demonstrations, and post-training pipelines for zero-tolerance manipulation tasks.

02

Vision-Language-Action Models

Studying action-attention specialization, task-factor disentanglement, and sim-to-real robustness for embodied foundation models.

03

Optimal Transport and Safe RL

Developing matrix-iterative optimal flow transport methods and capacity-flow views for safer, more sample-efficient reinforcement learning.

04

Generative Policy Learning

Combining geometric representations, diffusion actions, reward-manifold learning, and adaptive synchronization for contact-rich dexterous control.
